How to Play

Joining the OTC contest is simple — just buy and hold!

Here’s How it Works:

  • Every time you buy any amount of OTC, you’re automatically entered into the current 3-day contest. No sign-ups. No forms. Just buy and you’re in.

The Leaderboard

During each 3-day contest, our live leaderboard updates every 5 minutes showing the top buyers in key positions:

  • 1st Place
  • 5th Place
  • 10th Place
  • 20th Place
  • 30th Place
  • 50th Place
  • 100th Place

These spots matter — they might just win you big.

How Winners Are Picked

Throughout the 3-day contest, our system will take 10 random snapshots of the leaderboard.

Each snapshot will randomly select one of these positions to win:

  • → 1st, 5th, 10th, 20th, 30th, 50th, or 100th
  • Plus — 3 extra completely random wallets from any buyer!

That’s 10 chances to win every contest.

What Do You Win?

Each winner will automatically receive 3 million tokens of a mystery CTO (Community Takeover) coin.

At the end of the contest:

  • All winning snapshots are revealed.
  • The CTO coin is announced.
  • The OTC community begins its CTO push of the newly revealed coin.

Then the contest resets… and the next 3-day battle begins!

To Summarize:

  1. Buy OTC → You’re entered.
  2. Stay tuned to the live leaderboard.
  3. Hope your position (or wallet) gets picked.
  4. Win 3 million tokens of the next big CTO coin.
  5. Repeat every 5 days.
